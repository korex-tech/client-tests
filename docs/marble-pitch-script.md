**1 — Title**
# MARBLES × KOREX
Two products, one wallet, one universe of value.

**2 — The big idea**
## Marbles is its own product — and it's wired into the KOREX casino.
- Separate by design: because Marbles isn't gambling, we can advertise it anywhere a casino can't.
- Cross-promoted with KOREX on one shared wallet.
- The same marbles can be played and used on KOREX casino products.

**3 — The marble universe**
## A sport you can own.
- Marble Racing — 24 marbles, a new race every 3 minutes.
- Own, develop, trade and breed the marbles like real assets.
- Every race provably fair — replayable and auditable.

**4 — The cross-promotion that makes it special**
## The marble you own is also playable on the KOREX casino.
- Marble-built casino games — Crash, Plinko, physics-roulette — run on KOREX.
- Your asset isn't stuck in one place; it crosses into the casino.
- Two products that constantly feed each other.

**5 — The reach advantage**
## A front door a casino legally can't build.
- Marbles can run mainstream ads, app stores, influencers, social — broad and cheap.
- A licensed casino can't advertise like that.
- So Marbles acquires the audience; the casino monetises the intent.

**6 — Provably fair = the moat**
## Every casino asks you to trust the math. Ours lets you audit it.
- Deterministic, recorded, replayable.
- A fairness guarantee no traditional casino game can make.
- Trust as a feature, not a promise.

**7 — Own & grow**
## Buy a $5 marble. Race it into a legend.
- It grows the more it plays, up to its hidden potential.
- Top marbles earn a guaranteed value floor, backed by a ring-fenced reserve.
- Legends retire into a public Hall of Fame.

**8 — Breed (the economy that compounds)**
## Champions don't retire. They breed.
- Finite, tradable "dust" → cross bloodlines → dynasties, pedigree, provenance.
- The platform grows its own next generation of content — player-made.
- A second economy as deep as the first.

**9 — Revenue 1: the play**
## Every 3 minutes, money flows.
- 24 × $10 = a $240 pool → 10% rake = $24 to the platform.
- Multiply by every lobby, every 3 minutes, all day.
- Plus betting on races and marble casino games, at 10% margin.

**10 — Revenue 2: the economy**
## And the asset layer prints on its own.
- Marketplace (5% each side), discovery boxes, breeding/dust, certification.
- 100%-margin lines: account upgrades, cosmetics, season fees.
- Players pay to play — and pay to own.

**11 — Why it can't be copied**
## A marketable universe + the highest-margin product in gambling.
- Owned marble IP + a provably-fair sim + a player-owned economy.
- A generic casino can bolt on slots.
- It cannot bolt on a universe players own and advertise.

**12 — The big picture**
## Two products. One wallet. Cross-promoted.
- Marbles markets everywhere (it isn't gambling); KOREX casino monetises hardest.
- The same marbles play, pay, and cross into both.
- Built: the provably-fair engine, tracks, KOREX wallet/identity.
